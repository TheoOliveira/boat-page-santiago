Additional Details

Timeless, elegant, stylish and set up for entertaining, SHE’S A 10 is ready for her next owner to enjoy. Originally designed by Jon Bannenberg and Phil Curran, this 1989 164’ (49.9m) Oceanfast offers speed up to 30 knots at light load, agility and a shallow draft of 5’ 3” which is perfect for the Bahamas or other international cruising grounds. Sleeping 12 guests in 5 staterooms, the master is on the bridge deck with the 4 guest staterooms below. There is a media room on the main deck that can also convert into a 6th stateroom with shower and closet and storage. Previously known as Mystique, SHE’S A 10 has only had 3 owners and shows well. With recent fresh hull paint, W-6’s on the engines and endless upgrades.
